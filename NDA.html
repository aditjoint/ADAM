Confidential Information includes all proprietary data, documents, frameworks, assessments, business plans, financial information, proposals, pricing, reports, and any other information disclosed, whether orally, in writing, electronically, or otherwise, that is designated confidential or would reasonably be understood as confidential.

3. Exclusions from Confidential Information

Confidential Information does not include information that:

  • Is or becomes publicly known through no breach of this Agreement by the Receiving Party.

This Agreement remains in effect during the term of the relationship and for a period of five (5) years following termination or conclusion of any engagement or business relationship between the parties.
