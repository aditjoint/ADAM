Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

Last updated: May 27, 2025

This Non-Disclosure Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into by and between ADAM Consult, ADIT Joint LLP (“Disclosing Party”) and the recipient (“Receiving Party”) who agrees to the following terms in order to protect confidential and proprietary information.

1. Definition of Confidential Information

Confidential Information includes all proprietary data, documents, frameworks, assessments, business plans, financial information, proposals, pricing, reports, and any other information disclosed, whether orally, in writing, electronically, or otherwise, that is designated confidential or would reasonably be understood as confidential.

2. Obligations of Receiving Party

  • Maintain strict confidentiality of all Confidential Information received
