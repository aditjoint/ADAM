Last updated: April 15, 2025

2. Policy Scope

3. What is Whistleblowing?

Whistleblowing is the disclosure of information which relates to suspected wrongdoing or dangers at work, such as:

  • Criminal activity
  • Danger to health and safety
  • Damage to the environment
  • Failure to comply with any legal or professional obligation
  • Bribery or financial fraud
  • Miscarriages of justice
  • Deliberate concealment of any of the above

4. How to Raise a Concern

Raise concerns with your line manager or contact:

Whistleblowing Officer: adamconsultservice@gmail.com

Ethics Hotline: +91-8080280801

5. Confidentiality

We will make every effort to keep your identity confidential unless disclosure is legally required or necessary for investigation.

6. Protection for Whistleblowers

No retaliation will occur against those who report concerns in good faith. Malicious reports, however, may result in disciplinary action.

7. How We Will Respond

We will assess your report and follow up appropriately. We will keep you informed of progress and outcomes.
