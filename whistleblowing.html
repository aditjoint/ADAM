Whistleblower Policy (India)

Effective Date: April 15, 2025

1. Purpose

This Whistleblower Policy is in accordance with Section 177(9) of the Companies Act, 2013, and SEBI (LODR) Regulations for listed entities. It enables employees, vendors, and stakeholders to report unethical practices, fraud, or misconduct in confidence.

2. Applicability

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, interns, consultants, vendors, and directors associated with ADAM Consult across all locations in India.

3. Scope of Whistleblowing

Reportable matters include, but are not limited to:

  • Fraud or financial irregularities
  • Violation of laws or company policies
  • Corruption, bribery, or kickbacks
  • Misuse of company assets
  • Harassment or unethical conduct
  • Environmental or safety threats
  • Attempts to conceal any of the above

4. Reporting Mechanism

Complaints may be made anonymously or with identification. Reports can be verbal or written and must be made in good faith.

Whistleblower Officer: adamconsultservice@gmail.com

Hotline (India): +91-8080280801

5. Investigation Process

Upon receiving a concern, the Whistleblower Committee will evaluate and initiate an investigation. The findings will be recorded, and corrective actions will be taken if needed.

6. Confidentiality

The identity of the whistleblower will remain confidential unless disclosure is mandated by law or consent is given. All records are maintained securely.

7. Protection Against Retaliation

ADAM Consult prohibits retaliation against whistleblowers. Any victimization or harassment of the whistleblower will be considered a serious violation and subject to disciplinary action.

8. False Allegations

While genuine concerns are encouraged, knowingly false or malicious complaints may attract disciplinary action.

9. Legal References

  • Section 177 of the Companies Act, 2013
  • SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
  • POSH Act, 2013

10. Review and Update

This policy shall be reviewed annually and updated based on applicable regulations and internal needs.
