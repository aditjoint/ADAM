Whistleblowing Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2025

1. Purpose

ADAM Consult is committed to conducting business with honesty and integrity. We expect all staff to uphold the highest ethical standards. This policy encourages openness and ensures protection for individuals who raise genuine concerns about wrongdoing.

2. Policy Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, consultants, interns, part-time workers, and volunteers of ADAM Consult.

3. What is Whistleblowing?

Whistleblowing refers to reporting suspected wrongdoing such as:

  • Criminal activity
  • Danger to health and safety
  • Environmental harm
  • Legal or regulatory violations
  • Fraud or bribery
  • Concealment of any such acts

4. How to Raise a Concern

You may raise a concern with your reporting manager, or if not suitable, please contact:

Whistleblowing Officer: adamconsultservice@gmail.com

Ethics Hotline: +91-8080280801

5. Confidentiality

We aim to keep your identity confidential unless required by law. We will consult you if disclosure is necessary during the investigation.

6. Protection for Whistleblowers

If you act in good faith, you are protected from retaliation or victimization. However, malicious or knowingly false allegations may result in disciplinary action.

7. How We Will Respond

Once a concern is raised, we will assess it and initiate an investigation if necessary. You may be contacted for further input. All matters will be handled with due diligence and fairness.
