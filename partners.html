Utkal Electrotechnic

Focus: Electricity Distribution Engineering, Procurement & Project Development

About:

As a strategic partner, Utkal Electrotechnic supports turnkey execution, project structuring, and local compliance in international markets. Utkal Electrotechnic focuses are on the following domains namely –

Project Management and consultancy; specialize in delivering high-performance Transformer and Metering solutions that power critical infrastructure across industrial, commercial, and utility sectors. Our offerings span the full lifecycle — from design and manufacturing to deployment, calibration, and maintenance.

With a strong focus on efficiency, accuracy, and grid reliability, our product range includes:-

Distribution and Power Transformers

Energy Meters (AMR/AMI enabled, Smart Meters, Net Meters)

Metering Infrastructure & Data Analytics Solutions

Transformer Monitoring & Diagnostic Systems

Backed by a team of skilled engineers and industry-certified facilities, we ensure compliance with IS/IEC/ANSI standards, while integrating advanced digital technologies for real-time monitoring and remote asset management..

Key Contributions:

  • EPC & project deployment support
  • Local market enablement in Nigeria
  • Industrial design, tendering, and compliance
  • Global engineering alliances
