KMGX Energy Storage Systems

Technology Partner

KMGXx.com

Focus: Advanced Lithium Titanate (LTO) Battery Technology

About:

KMGX is an Australian innovator in sustainable energy storage solutions, specializing in high-performance Lithium Titanate (LTO) battery systems. Their battery packs offer exceptional safety, longevity (up to 20,000 cycles), and efficiency for residential, commercial, and grid applications.

Key Contributions:

  • LTO battery cell IP and design licensing
  • Technical co-development and product certification
  • Support for UN38.3, IEC, BIS compliance
  • Demo units, warranty structure, and pilot support
